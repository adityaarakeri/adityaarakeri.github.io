    About Aditya Arakeri

    Welcome to my corner of the web! I’m Aditya Arakeri, a seasoned software engineer with over a decade of experience blending technical expertise with strategic leadership. Throughout my career, I’ve cultivated a passion for crafting innovative solutions and leading dynamic teams to deliver exceptional results.

    Professional Journey:

    From steering cross-functional engineering teams at F5 Networks to shaping the development strategy at Shape Security, my journey has been marked by a commitment to excellence. As a hands-on leader, I’ve not only managed end-to-end projects but also optimized CI/CD pipelines, implemented automated testing, and navigated the intricacies of cloud computing platforms.

    Technical Proficiency:

    Proficient in languages such as Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, and Go, I’ve honed my skills in backend development and optimization. My expertise extends to database technologies, including SQL and NoSQL, and I’m well-versed in cloud computing platforms like AWS and GCP, alongside containerization technologies such as Docker and Kubernetes.

    Leadership and Innovation:

    Leading by example, I’ve successfully navigated strategic planning, back-end optimization, and cross-functional collaboration. My commitment to innovation is showcased through initiatives like migrating CI/CD pipelines for efficiency gains and implementing automated testing with 100% code coverage.

    Communication and Mentorship:

    Effective communication, both written and verbal, is at the core of my approach. I’ve conducted technical interviews, contributed to code reviews, and provided mentorship to junior developers, fostering a collaborative and growth-oriented environment.

    Areas of Expertise:

    Strategic Planning Back-end Development and Optimization Cross-functional Collaboration CI/CD Implementation Team Leadership and Management Cloud Computing (GCP, AWS)

    Let’s Connect:

    I’m excited to connect, collaborate, and explore new opportunities. Whether you’re interested in discussing innovative projects, exploring potential collaborations, or simply sharing insights, I’m always open to new conversations.
